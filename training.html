TRAINING

THE FOCUS ON RISKS TRAINING ADVANTAGE

Focus on Risks can provide and develop a range of training packages designed by WHS/OHS professionals who currently work with large, medium and small sized businesses to achieve their organisational goals. We also have co-provider status with Central Queensland TAFE and are able to provide businesses, and individuals, with accredited training programs under the Australian Qualifications Framework: Certificate IV OHS and Diploma OHS.

Our training packages are:

  •   Customised to suit individual business needs and risk profiles
  •   Focused on achieving business goals
  •   Based upon individual business processes, procedures and documents
  •   Designed for maximum participant interaction
  •   Cost effective (negotiated costs for materials, refreshments and venue, group discounts etc)

TRAINING SYSTEM DEVELOPMENT

We can assist you with determining your organisation’s WHS training needs including the key requirements and responsibilities of each position, based on your organisational risk profile and the inherent needs of the organisation.

  • Training Needs Analysis
  • Training Matrix
  • Inclusion of WHS Responsibilities in Position Descriptions
  • Development of Training Plans
  • Evaluation of Training Programs

If you cannot see the training you require above, JUST ASK, as we have experienced consultants who have worked across a large range of disciplines and diverse occupational streams. They will be able to assist you in either developing a training program to suit your needs or obtaining the expertise you need through our contacts and sub- contractors.
