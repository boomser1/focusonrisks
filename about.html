About Us

Our team at Focus On Risks have extensive leadership, management and hands on industry experience working for and within the Private and Government sectors, including both large (65,000+ employees), medium and small organisations.

Our consultants are tertiary qualified and Del is an Accredited Auditor (Exemplar Global – Lead Auditor OHSMS & Compliance, Plant and Injury Management and QLD Self Insurance OHS Auditor).

We have experience across a range of industries such as property, health, education, emergency services, manufacturing, construction, retail, aviation, mining and quarries, forestry, water, professional services (i.e. engineering, design, legal, financial), and disability services.

We began Focus on Risks to provide business with a unique “One Stop WHS/OHS and HR Service’. We value positive relationships and commit to our clients for the long term. We believe “YOUR SUCCESS IS OUR SUCCESS.”
