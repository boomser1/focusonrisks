WORK HEALTH AND SAFETY RISK ASSESSMENTS, INCIDENT MANAGEMENT AND INVESTIGATIONS RISK MANAGEMENT

Risk management is an integral part of any business and there are legislative obligations to ensure effective risk management strategies are in place. Our consultants are very experienced across a broad range of organisations, and can support you to identify and assess hazards, systems and processes (including human and cultural factors), prioritise risks, and provide practical and cost effective solutions to ensure compliance and the minimisation or elimination of risks.

  • Strategic Identification of Hazards
  • Development of Risk Registers: Corporate, Divisional, Regional and Work Unit
  • Dangerous Goods and Hazardous Substances
  • Plant and Equipment
  • Working at Heights
  • Manual Handling and Ergonomics including workstation assessments
  • Fire and Emergency Preparedness
  • Traffic Management
  • Heat Stress
  • Exursions and Travel
  • Asbestos & Hazardous Building Material
  • Driver-Vehicle Safety including mobile workplace vans
  • Occupational Hygiene Assessments i.e. noise, lighting, air monitoring, mould etc
  • Cooling Towers
  • Occupational Violence
  • Security
  • First Aid
  • Environmental Risks
  • Public Areas, Parks and Clubhouses
  • Children's Playgrounds
  • Working Alone and Remote Area Work
  • Asbestos Abatement Management

INCIDENT MANAGEMENT AND INVESTIGATIONS

Unfortunately, incidents may still happen and we recognise that its important to manage an incident appropriately and to undertake an investigation to determine root cause. This helps to prevent another person (employee, contractor and visitor) sustaining a similar or same type of injury, and ensures that appropriate systems are put in place to minimise future costs and losses.

  • Incident Management including liaison with the WHS Inspectorate
  • Safety Incidents and Near Misses
  • Vehicle & Plant Incidents
  • Grievance
  • Fraud
  • WorkCover
  • Security
